How It Works

Backlog Booster uses Cheapshark's API to make calls on the cheapest games - and gives you a suggestion for the next one to add to your backlog. On the Home page, a price floor is randomly generated and the price ceiling is fixed at fifty dollars. Next, an array returns - and a random index generates from the Javascript running behind the scenes. A simple way to figure out what game to play next - leave it up to chance!

Thank you to Cheapshark for their easily accesible API!
